    Enjoy Your Time in Nature, Whatever the Season

    At Manning Park Resort, there’s always something fun to do. Whether the weather is hot or cold, or somewhere in between, we’ve got the ideal mix of activities so you can enjoy spending time out in nature.

    winter
    spring and fall
    summer

    Make the most of optimal powder conditions

    The days may be shorter, but there’s no less fun to be had at Manning Park. Spend your time out in nature skiing or snowboarding as you enjoy 140 acres of skiable terrain. And, if you’re feeling adventurous, why not try Nordic skiing, ice skating, snowshoeing or tobogganing for something a bit different.

    The park is situated in a transition zone in southern British Columbia between coastal rainforest and the semi-arid Okanagan. The high precipitation on the coast is balanced by the drier climate in the region to the east of the Cascades so that Manning Park Resort has a high average snowfall (743cm annually) and ideal snow conditions from December until early spring.

    Temperatures in winter average -2°C during the day, and -9°C at night, so remember to dress warmly to enjoy your day on the slopes.

    Our visitors during spring and fall make the most of the moderate temperatures, taking the opportunity to get out into nature while the temperatures are cool yet comfortable. This is a popular time for hiking, mountain bike riding and stargazing.

    Many of our regulars take advantage of the shoulder seasons when the resort is a bit quieter. They choose these times to enjoy time out in nature to enjoy the space and peace. In many cases, you can hike for hours without seeing another person.

    Average daytime temperatures in spring are 7°C, and in fall they are 9°C.

    In summer, seemingly endless days stretch out ahead of you that can be filled with a wide range of activities including camping, hiking, biking, horseback riding and exploring nature.

    The opportunities to relax and unwind at Manning Park Resort are limitless so we invite you to join us, take in a deep breath of fresh mountain air and reconnect with nature during your time with us.
    Manning Park weather in summer is mild with an average summertime temperature of 18°C in the day and 5°C at night; so you’ll be able to get the most out of your time with us.
